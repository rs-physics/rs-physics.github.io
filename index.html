Welcome!

This website contains a repository of resources made to help teach and revise GCSE and A-level physics made by myself. Please note that these were designed with the AQA specification in mind, but can probably be used for general physics as well.

A significant portion of the videos were made during the COVID pandemic, and have been uploaded now for general use. I may add some more videos, simulations and other resources over time.

Explore, and learn, hopefully some of these resources will be of use.
